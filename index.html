Schedule Notary Services

We offer three types of notarization services. Here's how they work:

  • In-Person Notarization: You meet with a notary face-to-face to sign documents. This includes traditional physical signatures or IPEN (In-Person Electronic Notarization), where you sign electronically in person. Price: $15
  • Remote Online Notarization (RON): You meet with a notary via a secure video platform and electronically sign documents. Ideal for clients who cannot meet in person. Price: $30
