About Us

Kreated Notion Designs provides creative graphic design services including logos, printing, and custom designs. We also offer notary services both in-person and online.

Our Services

  • Custom Logo Design
  • Flyers & Business Cards
  • Website Graphics
  • Print & Digital Designs
  • In-Person Notary: Traditional notary service where you meet face-to-face with the notary to sign and notarize your documents.
  • IPEN (In-Person Electronic Notarization): A modern alternative where you meet the notary in person, but sign the documents electronically using a secure digital platform.
  • RON (Remote Online Notarization): Fully remote notary service that allows you to complete the entire notarization process online via secure audio-video technology. Perfect for clients who are out of state, traveling, or need quick and contactless service.
